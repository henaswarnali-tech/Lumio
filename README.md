##  What Is LUMIO?

**LUMIO** is a free, offline-capable, gamified self-learning mobile platform designed for underserved children and adults worldwide — from zero literacy to fully employable skills.

Millions of children and adults around the world — in rural Bangladesh, sub-Saharan Africa, remote India, and beyond — are locked out of quality education not because they lack intelligence, but because the system was never built for them.

LUMIO changes that. Permanently. Freely.

-  **Mobile-first** — works on any basic Android smartphone
-  **Offline-capable** — works without internet after initial download
-  **Gamified** — designed like a game, not a textbook
-  **Neuro-inclusive** — built for every type of learner including ADHD, dyslexia, and non-traditional learners
-  **Adaptive placement** — every learner starts at their own level, not from zero
-  **Zero to skilled** — from complete zero literacy to professional employable skills
-  **Blockchain-verified certificates** — tamper-proof credentials for jobs and university globally
-  **Fully open source** — forever free, forever community-owned

##  The Problem

- **300 million+ children** globally are out of school
- **750 million adults** are functionally illiterate
- Existing free platforms assume basic literacy and stable internet
- Traditional education is built for memorisation, not skill-building
- Child labourers, rural communities, and neurodivergent learners are systematically excluded
- Even "free" education is not designed for the way many brains actually work

LUMIO is built specifically for the people every other platform leaves behind.

##  The Solution

A complete educational journey in one free mobile app:

```
STAGE 0 — PLACEMENT
    Smart diagnostic quiz → finds your exact level → you start there

STAGE 1 — FOUNDATION (Grades 1–5 equivalent)
    Zero literacy → Reading, writing, numeracy, critical thinking, digital literacy

STAGE 2 — PATHWAYS (Grades 6–10 equivalent)
    Student chooses interest path/s — paths can be combined
    Technical · Health · Business · Creative · Agriculture · Education

STAGE 3 — SKILLS (Professional Level)
    Each path leads to: Self-Employment track · Job track · Business track
    Blockchain-verified certificates issued on completion
```

---

##  Built for Every Brain

LUMIO detects and adapts to how each person learns:

- **Neurotypical mode** — standard interface
- **ADHD mode** — shorter sessions, more rewards, reduced visual noise
- **Dyslexia mode** — OpenDyslexic font, increased spacing, audio-first
- **Low literacy mode** — icon and audio-led, minimal text
- **Any learner can switch modes at any time in settings**

---

##  Adaptive Placement — No One Starts at the Wrong Level

On first open, LUMIO runs a short diagnostic:
- Visual questions for zero-literacy users
- Progressively harder questions for higher levels
- Detects approximate grade level in under 5 minutes
- Places student at the right starting point automatically
- No account or email required — works on device ID + optional PIN

##  Technology Stack

| Layer | Technology | Why |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| Mobile App | Flutter | One codebase for iOS + Android |
| Animations | Lottie + Rive | Lightweight, beautiful, interactive |
| Database | Firebase Firestore | Free tier, offline sync, scales globally |
| Auth | Firebase Auth | Anonymous + PIN — no email needed |
| Certificates | Blockcerts + IPFS | Tamper-proof, no cryptocurrency |
| Offline | Flutter offline persistence | Download once, learn forever |
| UI Design | Figma | Open design files included |

---

## 🏆 Certificates That Mean Something

When a student passes a grade or skill:

1. Certificate generated with name and achievement
2. Unique record written to blockchain — permanent, tamper-proof
3. QR code on certificate verifiable by any employer or university globally
4. Cannot be faked. Cannot be deleted. Belongs to the student forever.

##  Sustainability

LUMIO is built on a simple belief: the tools that serve the most 
vulnerable people in the world should be owned by everyone 
and controlled by no one.

That is why LUMIO is open source. Forever.

**For contributors:**
Every line of code, every lesson written, every translation 
added belongs to the global community — not to any company 
or individual. Your contribution outlives you. It reaches 
children you will never meet, in places you may never visit.

**For the project to keep growing:**
LUMIO's ongoing development is supported through deployment 
partnerships with NGOs, schools, and governments who use 
LUMIO to serve their communities. These partnerships fund 
full-time development without ever charging the learners 
this platform exists to serve.

Learners always free. Always.
