##  What Is LUMIO?

**LUMIO** is a free, offline-capable, gamified self-learning mobile platform for underserved children and adults worldwide — from zero literacy to fully employable skills.

Millions of people are locked out of quality education not because they lack intelligence, but because the system was never built for them. Child labourers, rural communities, neurodivergent learners, adults who never finished school — every platform that claims to serve them still assumes they have internet, a teacher, and a brain that works one specific way.

LUMIO was built for everyone that assumption leaves behind.

-  **Mobile-first** — works on any basic Android smartphone
-  **Offline-capable** — download once, learn forever
-  **Gamified** — designed like a game, not a textbook
-  **Neuro-inclusive** — adapts to ADHD, dyslexia, low literacy, and neurotypical learners
-  **Adaptive placement** — every learner starts at their own level automatically
-  **Zero to skilled** — from absolute zero literacy to professional employable skills
-  **Blockchain-verified certificates** — tamper-proof credentials recognised globally
-  **Fully open source** — forever free, forever community-owned

##  How LUMIO Works

LUMIO is a **self-learning app.** Students learn alone, at their own pace, with no teacher required.

```
STAGE 0 — PLACEMENT
    Short adaptive quiz → finds exact learning level → student starts there
    No wrong answer. No judgment. Just a starting point.

STAGE 1 — FOUNDATION  (Grades 1–5 equivalent)
    Zero literacy → critical thinking, digital literacy, life skills
    EQ, IQ, mental wellbeing, body safety woven throughout every grade

STAGE 2 — PATHWAYS  (Grades 6–10 equivalent depth)
    Student chooses 1 or 2 interest paths
    Technical · Business · Creative · Health · Agriculture · Education
    Paths can combine into merged skill tracks

STAGE 3 — PROFESSIONAL SKILLS
    Short focused courses directly tied to employment
    Each course ends with a blockchain-verified certificate
    Three tracks per skill: Self-Employment · Job-Seeking · Entrepreneurship
```

##  The Educator Account — For Teachers and NGO Workers

LUMIO is self-learning. Students do not need a teacher.

But sometimes students are part of a group — a community learning centre, an NGO literacy programme, a rural school. The adult responsible for that group needs a way to see how their learners are doing without sitting next to each one.

That is what the **Educator Account** is for.

**What an educator account does:**
- See all students in their group and their progress in one place
- See which students are stuck and which grades they are on
- See which students have not opened the app recently
- Download and print certificates for students they supervise
- Get simple weekly reports on their group's progress

**What an educator account does NOT do:**
- Control what a student sees or learns
- Change the student's experience in any way
- Collect any private student information beyond progress data

**The student's learning journey is always their own.**

## 🏆 Certificates That Mean Something

When a student completes a grade or skill:

1. A certificate is generated with their name and achievement
2. A permanent record is written to the blockchain — tamper-proof, forever
3. A QR code on the certificate allows any employer or university to verify it instantly
4. It cannot be faked. It cannot be deleted. It belongs to the student forever.

LUMIO certificates reflect genuine mastery — not performance under pressure, not memorisation, not attendance. Real demonstrated skill.

##  How LUMIO Stays Free — The Sustainability Model

**Students use LUMIO completely free. Forever. No exceptions. No hidden costs.**

LUMIO sustains itself through the people and organisations that serve students — not from the students themselves:

**Educator Accounts — Small monthly fee**
Teachers, NGO workers, and community educators who manage groups of students pay a small monthly fee for their dashboard. The students they supervise always use LUMIO free.

**Community Deployment — Paid partnerships**
Governments, NGOs, and international organisations that want to deploy LUMIO for their specific community — with their local language, local context, and their own branding — pay for that deployment and ongoing support.

**Donations and Sponsorships**
Individuals and companies who believe in the mission support LUMIO's development directly. No advertiser ever influences what LUMIO teaches or how.

The goal is a platform that earns enough to keep developing — without ever charging the people it was built to serve.
